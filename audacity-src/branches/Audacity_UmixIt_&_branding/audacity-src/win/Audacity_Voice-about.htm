About Audacity_Voice

 

Audacity_Voice is a free, customised version of Audacity 1.2.6.

 

As its name suggests, we have modified Audacity with the specific needs of voice recording in mind. Enhanced visual presentation of sequential files is perhaps the most obvious modification, but there are dozens of changes which speed up many of the tasks involved in voice authoring. The "Email" command sends selected voice files directly to the user's email app as an attachment.

 

Audacity_Voice is made possible by the entire Audacity Team. Audacity_Voice modifications were made by Vaughan Johnson. All those who, with microphone in hand, wish to enhance folks' computer experiences with effective voice recordings, will no doubt join me in expressing gratitude to these guys!
